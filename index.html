How to Get Value from a Newsletter

To extract the most value from Dopious (or any high‑quality newsletter), adopt a practical reading routine:

  • Read with intent: Identify one idea per issue you can test immediately.

  • Discuss and apply: Share useful items with your team and convert them into small experiments.
  • Measure outcomes: Track the impact of any tip you implement so you can iterate on what works.
